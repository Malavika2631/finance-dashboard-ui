ABOUT

Finlytics is a modern financial dashboard designed to help users track, analyze, and manage their finances with ease. It provides a clear overview of income, expenses, and savings through intuitive visualizations and a user-friendly interface. With features like expense tracking, real-time analytics, and insightful reports, Finlytics empowers users to make smarter financial decisions and stay in control of their money.
